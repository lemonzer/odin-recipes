Easy To Make Popcorn Great For Weight Loss

Popcorns are great for weight lose as they are filling, high in fiber, low in calories, and low cost.

Ingredients

  • Popcorn kernals (3 tablespoons)
  • Vegetable oil or any oil of your choice (2 tablespoons)
  • Salt (lightly to your liking)

Steps

  1. Place popcorn kernal and oil in pot
  2. Heat pot and cover
  3. After popping, sprinkle salt for taste
